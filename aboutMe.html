A little bit about me..


My name is Anmol Raina, and I am currently a senior studying Computer Science and Philosophy at Carleton College in Northfield, MN. I have a strong passion for coding and breaking down complex problems. I have been interested in programming for 4-5 years now and enjoy learning new languages and techniques.

Technically, I would describe myself as a Full-Stack developer, well versed in several programming languages. I have recently developed an interest in web development and want to become an accomplised web guru.

I have been looking for ways to incorporate my aptitude for developing code into creative industries, especially advertising and marketing. My passion for creating and discussing ideas manifests in my (sometimes rather sad) attempts at creative writing and to solve philosophical questions in new ways. This is what got me interested in game programming and now has brought me to the gateway of web development. I want to learn about new technologies to produce efficient code while fostering my imagination and urge for building something that is my own. I am excited to work in a field that is an intersection of web development (backend+frontend) and advertising/marketing.
